WHAT WE DO..


だれか PANTRY (For-Anyone-Pantry) is an organized community pantry that helps out
with the distribution of food products to those who are in need.

These goods are not our own but, from kind-hearted individuals and organizations
who wants to extend a helping hand but don't know where to start.
